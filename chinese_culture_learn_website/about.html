About

This website is to show the different aspects of Chinese culture through video recordings of presentations accompanied by a series of pictures. These culture presentations mainly target the AP Chinese student audience but is also a great way for anyone who is curious to learn more! They are all recorded in the Chinese language and have a short description in both English and Chinese. In addition, they are organized into multiple categories (click on the text that pops up to go there!):

Holidays
Cuisine
Famous Figures
Architecture
Ethnic Groups
Four Great Classics
