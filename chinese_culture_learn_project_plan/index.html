Project Plan

Overview and Intended Audience

My goal with this website is to revamp one of the older websites I made u sing Wix which was to share culture presentation videos on Chinese. This means that it will basically contain a lot of videos with descriptions on what the video will contain. I will also want to include some sort of "form" that can be used to like/dislike the video. I will be organizing the videos into categories as well, which may lead to sub-pages being created. The intended audience is Chinese learners at my old Chinese school to learn more about culture-related topics for their presentations.

CSS and Look

The color palette for this will most likely be a combination of yellows, reds, as well as black and white to suit the theme of my website. My font will most likely be a typical and easy-to-read font like Palatino, Sofia Pro, and Barlow to make it look clean and simple to understand.

SEO Keywords

The keywords I planned to use were "Chinese culture", "Ethnic cultures", "learning Chinese", etc. Some websites I found with these keywords were China Highlights and Britannica.

User Experience Improvement and Probabiblity Increase

I will also include a title tag, website description, image alts, viewport, and more to increase traffic and improve User Experience. I'm also thinking of adding a couple mini animations as well to keep the user engaged in the website as they hover/scroll on the website.
